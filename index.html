PROSPECT & PROSPECT PTM: A Collection of Proteomics Datasets for Machine Learning and Deep Learning

PROSPECT and PROSPECT PTM (PROteometools SPECTrum compendium) are large annotated datasets, leveraging the raw data from ProteomeTools [1] [2], including unmodified and modified peptide sequences (with Post-Translational Modifications, PTMs).
